About us

Our Story

Founded in 2005, Cozy Haven Stay has grown from a single property to a collection of luxury hotels across the globe. Our mission is to provide exceptional hospitality experiences that create lasting memories.

With over 50 properties in 20 countries, we pride ourselves on our attention to detail, personalized service, and commitment to sustainability. Our team of dedicated professionals works tirelessly to ensure every guest feels at home.

Whether you're traveling for business or pleasure, Grand Horizon Hotels offers the perfect blend of comfort, style, and convenience to make your stay unforgettable.
