About us

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Cozy Haven Stay began as a passion project between travel enthusiasts who wanted to simplify hotel bookings while ensuring authentic hospitality experiences. What started as a small platform connecting travelers with boutique hotels has now grown into India's most trusted hotel booking service.

Today, we partner with over 25,000 properties across 500+ destinations in India and Southeast Asia. Our unique algorithm matches travelers with accommodations that perfectly fit their preferences, budget, and travel purpose - whether it's business, leisure, or family vacations.

What sets us apart is our Verified Stays program, where our team personally inspects each property for quality assurance. We've introduced innovative features like real-time availability checks, instant confirmations, and 24/7 customer support to make your travel planning seamless.

At Cozy Haven Stay, we don't just book rooms - we craft experiences. Our local travel experts curate special packages and insider tips to help you discover the true essence of every destination.
