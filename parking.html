About Our Parking Facilities

At Cozy Haven Stay, we understand the importance of convenient and secure parking for our guests. Enjoy our complimentary parking amenities throughout your stay.

24/7 Security

Our parking area is monitored around the clock with CCTV cameras and security personnel.

Ample Space

Over 200 parking spots available, including accessible spaces near all entrances.

EV Charging

We offer 10 dedicated electric vehicle charging stations (first come, first served).

Valet Service

Optional valet parking available for an additional fee ($15/night).

Parking Guidelines

  • Parking is free for registered hotel guests only
  • Please display your parking permit on the dashboard
  • Oversized vehicles should contact the front desk
  • No in/out privileges - one vehicle per room

For any parking-related inquiries during your stay, please contact our front desk at extension 555 from your room phone.
